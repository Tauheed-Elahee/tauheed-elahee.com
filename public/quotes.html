Quotes

This page contains quotes I find from various places I want to share with everyone.

These quotes are, to paraphrase Voltaire, serve only to please myself and which suit my tasts. Do not consider these quotes to be admirable because famous people said or wrote them, just as interesting pieces of stand alone text. I will however do my best to provide a source so that active readers may see the quotes in their appropriate context.

Fools have a habit of believing that everything written by a famous author is admirable. For my part I read only to please myself and like only what suits my taste.

Voltaire
